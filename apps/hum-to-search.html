Hum to Search

Google — Creative Engineering

A real-time audio visualizer built at Google for the Hum to Search feature. Creative engineering combining audio analysis with expressive motion graphics.

When you hum, whistle, or sing into Google Search, the visualizer responds in real-time — translating your voice into fluid, dynamic graphics that feel alive. The challenge was making something technically precise feel organic and delightful.

Created in collaboration with Motion Designer Adam Grabowski.
