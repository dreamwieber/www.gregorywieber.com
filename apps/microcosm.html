Microcosm is a 'sound toy' app I created in 2012. It takes the idea of a snow globe that you can tilt and shake and combines it with Granular Synthesis, which takes sounds and explodes them into what can be best described as sonic clouds.

The 3D grain cloud was created in OpenGL, and uses the iPhone's accelerometer and gyroscope to control a verlet physics routine. Spatial 3D audio creates an immersive effect when listening with headphones. The whole experience was designed to be zen-like / meditative.
