Vibescape

App — Apple Vision Pro

From the moment Apple Vision Pro was announced, I knew I wanted in. In the months leading up to release, I worked on building Vibescape, which launched as a day one app and has since been featured numerous times by Apple.

Vibescape has evolved from an app focused on Spatial Video of the Pacific Northwest, to one that is focused on pushing the boundaries of what immersive environments can be on Vision Pro.
