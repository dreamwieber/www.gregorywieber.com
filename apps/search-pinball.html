Google — Easter Egg

In collaboration with Google's Delight team, we created an easter egg game inside the Google Search app for iPhone that actually landed on Fox News's top games of the year list. As the lead creative technologist on the project, I helped develop the concept, design, and implementation of the game — a kind of fusion of Breakout and Pinball that plays with the design language of the app.
