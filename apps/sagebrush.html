SageBrush

App — AI Painter, iPad

SageBrush was one of the first apps that let you draw with AI, rather than prompting alone. The idea was to create a tool that enabled artists to express themselves through this new medium, and to make AI-creation feel less like a slot machine, and more like a familiar tool.
