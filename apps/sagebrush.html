SageBrush

App — AI Painter, iPad

The simplest way to create with AI. Sketch with Apple Pencil, describe what you imagine, and watch your drawing transform. An AI painting app built at Apposite.

SageBrush puts creative control back in your hands. Instead of typing prompts into a black box, you sketch your composition, guide the AI with a description, and refine the result. It's a collaboration between human intuition and machine imagination.
