I'm a designer, creative engineer, and indie developer. In 2010 I created Polychord for iPad — an award-winning music app used by musicians around the world. After eight years at Google as a creative engineer, I returned to indie development at Apposite, building spatial apps like Vibescape for Vision Pro.
